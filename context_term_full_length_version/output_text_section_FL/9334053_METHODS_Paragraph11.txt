2.11. Statistical Analysis. SPSS 22.0 software was used for the statistical analyses in present study. Each experiment was performed for at least three times. All data were demonstrated as the mean +- standard derivation (SD). Student's t-test was applied for the comparison of two groups, while ANOVA was applied to examine the differences of three or more groups. Data was considered to indicate statistical significance with two-sided P < 0.05.
